Research

Exposure to NSSI Content in The Media and Its Indirect Influences on Proper Help Seeking

This study examines how adolescents feel about sharing personal feelings with adults and how adolescents themselves perceive non-suicidal self-injurious behavior and the correlation between depression and other possible factors that may relate to self-injurious thought. The ultimate purpose is to see how exposure to NSSI content in the media and its indirect influences on proper help seeking.
